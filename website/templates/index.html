Enter the name of a Steemit.com account that you wish to exchange upvotes.
This is the percentage of your upvote that you'd like to exchange. Exchanges are always negotiated using the inviter's upvote. The person you invite will have the opportunity to barter with you before they accept and it will always be your (inviter's) upvote that is being bartered on.
This is the ratio between your upvote and the person's upvote that you are inviting. This allows whales and minnows to exchange unequal upvotes! Just like the percentage, it's your upvote that is being adjusted, which is the number on the left of the ratio. You can adjust the ratio from 0.001 (one thousandth of your upvote compared to the person you're inviting) all the way to 1000 (your upvote is one thousand times the other upvote).
